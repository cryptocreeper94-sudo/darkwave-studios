Marketing Description (Full):
Happy Eats is a zone-based food truck ordering platform serving the Nashville I-24 Corridor. It connects local food truck vendors with hungry customers through a streamlined batch ordering system with an 11 AM daily cutoff. The platform features vendor registration and management, zone-based delivery routing, and blockchain-verified transaction records through the Trust Layer ecosystem. Happy Eats integrates with the ecosystem's cross-platform affiliate system (he- prefix affiliate IDs), sharing a revenue model with TL Driver Connect — charging a 15% platform fee on all vendor orders with affiliate revenue share coming out of that fee. The platform supports vendor referral bonuses ($20 per qualified vendor) and tiered ongoing revenue share (1% Starter tier for 1-9 referrals, 2% Builder tier for 10+).

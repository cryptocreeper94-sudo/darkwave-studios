VERDARA — Complete App Description
One-Liner
Verdara is the ultimate outdoor recreation super-app — identify species, discover trails, plan trips, book campgrounds, and shop a verified marketplace, all in one place.

Marketing Description
Verdara puts the entire outdoors in your pocket. Whether you're a weekend hiker, a seasoned angler, a professional arborist, or someone who just wants to know what bird is singing outside your window, Verdara is built for you. Browse a living catalog of 125+ verified outdoor locations across 41 states, identify plants and wildlife with a snap of your camera, plan multi-day adventures with gear checklists and weather forecasts, compare prices across 90+ outdoor retailers, and tap into a blockchain-verified wood economy marketplace. Verdara is developed by DarkWave Studios as App #28 in the DarkWave Trust Layer ecosystem.

Feature Set
Core Features
Species Identification — Snap a photo of any tree, plant, fish, or wildlife and get instant identification with confidence scores and detailed species profiles. Also supports audio identification for animal sounds.
Outdoor Catalog — A continuously updated, encyclopedia-quality nationwide catalog of 125+ outdoor locations across 41 states, with detailed descriptions, activities, amenities, maps, and user reviews. Designed as a living catalog that grows over time.
Trip Planner — Build multi-day itineraries with route planning, gear checklists, and integrated weather forecasts from Open-Meteo.
Campground Booking — Full reservation system for campgrounds with availability search, site details, and booking management.
GPS Trail Tracking — Real-time trail tracking with permission-gated location access.
Price Compare — Template-driven universal comparison engine spanning 90+ affiliate retailers across 18 outdoor gear categories.
Wild Edibles & Natural Medicine — Database of wild plants with foraging guides, edibility info, and medicinal uses, integrated with species identification.
Marketplace & Commerce
Wood Economy Marketplace — Buy and sell lumber with Stripe checkout, seller management, and vendor verification through TrustShield blockchain certification.
Subscription Tiers — Free Explorer, Outdoor Explorer, Craftsman Pro, Arborist Starter, Arborist Business, and Arborist Enterprise tiers with feature gating and Stripe Checkout integration.
Professional Tools
Arborist Pro — Client management, job scheduling, and invoicing for tree care professionals.
Arbora (App #29) — Standalone PWA at /arbora/* for arborist business management featuring CRM, estimates, job management, invoicing, calendar, crew management, and inventory tracking.
Communication & Community
Signal Chat — Real-time WebSocket chat with Trust Layer SSO, channel-based messaging, and user presence.
Verdara Journal (Blog) — AI-assisted, SEO-optimized blog system with public listing, detail pages, and admin editor.
User Reviews — Community-driven reviews and ratings on catalog locations.
Intelligence & Assistance
Evergreen Assistant — Floating chatbot accessible from any page, powered by GPT-4o-mini, for answering outdoor recreation questions.
Developer Portal — Roadmap, affiliate network overview, and business suite preview.
Visual & UX
Cinematic Hero — Rotating flyover video slideshow with smooth crossfade transitions and dot navigation indicators on the landing page.
Dark Mode Default — Earthy green, slate, and amber color palette with glassmorphism cards and bento grid layouts.
60fps Animations — Framer Motion throughout for smooth transitions and micro-interactions.
Mobile-First PWA — Bottom tab navigation on mobile, sidebar on desktop, fully responsive.
Photorealistic Imagery — Every card and location features real photography, no gray placeholders.

Tech Stack
Frontend: React 18 + TypeScript, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI, Framer Motion, Wouter (routing), TanStack React Query
Backend: Express.js + TypeScript
Database: PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM
Authentication: Custom email/password with bcrypt hashing, cookie-based sessions, Resend for email verification, Trust Layer SSO
Payments: Stripe (checkout, subscriptions, marketplace)
Email: Resend for transactional delivery
Weather: Open-Meteo API
AI/ML: OpenAI GPT-4o (species identification via Vision API), GPT-4o-mini (Evergreen assistant), Whisper (audio identification)
Hosting: Replit with autoscale deployment
